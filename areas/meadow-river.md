|-------------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Some (15-30) ‚Äî jugs, easy overhangs, traverses [UNVERIFIED] | Many (20-40) ‚Äî overhanging faces, compression, pinches [UNVERIFIED] | Some (10-25) ‚Äî steep compression, roof pulls, powerful sequences [UNVERIFIED] |

## Group Suitability

Good for all levels. V4-V6 density is highest ‚Äî great for intermediate climbers. Hard climbers have Some V7-V10+ options on steep, powerful rock. Beginners have enough V0-V3 to stay busy. Full day area.

## Weather Resilience

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Rating** | Good |
| **Shelter** | Many problems on heavily overhanging faces/roofs ‚Äî stay dry during rain. **Hard problems (V7+) are predominantly sheltered** ‚Äî the steep compression and roof lines that define hard climbing here are the same features that provide rain protection. |
| **Seepage** | Some on crack systems and horizontal breaks after heavy/prolonged rain [UNVERIFIED] |
| **Drying** | Overhanging = already dry. Vertical = 1-3 hrs with sun+wind. Steep nature means faster drying overall. |
| **Wind** | Moderate ‚Äî river corridor funnels wind, aids drying |
| **Rain verdict** | Yes ‚Äî overhanging problems climbable during active rain. Best rain-day bouldering option in the region. |

## Sectors

### Main Area / Roadside [UNVERIFIED]

| Rain Resilience | V0-V3 | V4-V6 | V7-V10+ | Shelter |
|-----------------|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| Good | Some | Many | Some | Roadside = wind-exposed but overhanging faces stay dry |

Most accessible sector. Freestanding blocks visible from the road. Shortest approach ‚Äî park at pulloff and walk to boulders. Wind-exposed due to open roadside position, but overhanging rock provides rain shelter.

### River Boulders [UNVERIFIED]

| Rain Resilience | V0-V3 | V4-V6 | V7-V10+ | Shelter |
|-----------------|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| Good | Some | Many | Some | Some natural shelter from river bank terrain |

Along the Meadow River bank, short walk from pulloffs. May need creek crossing to reach some boulders. Good spread across all grades with natural shelter provided by river bank terrain.

### Upstream / Upper Meadow [UNVERIFIED]

| Rain Resilience | V0-V3 | V4-V6 | V7-V10+ | Shelter |
|-----------------|-------|-------|---------|---------|
| Fair (less documented) | Few | Few | Few | Limited ‚Äî less documented shelter |

Less developed sector with additional potential. Limited problems and less documentation means shelter and drying characteristics are not well established.

## Climbing Style

Nuttall sandstone. Predominantly overhanging ‚Äî steep faces, compression, roofs, aretes. The rock is characteristically hard and featured with crimps, pinches, slopers, and horizontal rails. Powerful, athletic climbing on bullet-hard sandstone. Landing zones vary from flat roadside dirt to uneven riverside talus.
