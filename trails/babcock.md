## Nearby Pairing: Glade Creek Grist Mill

The iconic Glade Creek Grist Mill is located inside Babcock State Park, approximately 1 minute drive from the main parking areas. Even if trail conditions are poor for riding, the mill is worth the visit as one of West Virginia's most photographed landmarks. Note that the mill building may be closed in winter, but the exterior and surrounding scenery remain accessible. [UNVERIFIED]
