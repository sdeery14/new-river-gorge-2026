## Quick Decision Guide

- **Dry + cold + frozen ground** ‚Üí [Arrowhead Trails](arrowhead.md) ‚Äî closest, free, purpose-built singletrack. Ride early on frozen ground before midday thaw.
- **Wet / muddy** ‚Üí **Skip riding.** Go boulder instead ‚Äî see [Bouldering Areas](../areas/README.md). Riding muddy trails causes lasting damage. Respect the trails.
- **Want the best trails** ‚Üí [Summit Bechtel Reserve](summit-bechtel.md) ‚Äî by far the most trail mileage and variety. BUT: private BSA facility, public access uncertain. **Must call (304) 465-2800 to verify access before driving.**
- **Short on time** ‚Üí [Arrowhead Trails](arrowhead.md) ‚Äî 5-10 min from town, ride in under an hour.
- **Scenic light ride** ‚Üí [Babcock State Park](babcock.md) ‚Äî old roads and railroad grades through state park. Pair with Glade Creek Grist Mill visit. Not purpose-built MTB.
- **All trails unrideable** ‚Üí Pivot to an all-bouldering day. See [Bouldering Areas](../areas/README.md). This is expected in February ‚Äî mountain biking in mid-February WV is opportunistic, not guaranteed.

## February Riding Tips

*For freeze/thaw timeline details and how to pair morning rides with afternoon bouldering, see [Conditions Guide ‚Üí Freeze/Thaw Guide](../conditions/README.md#freezethaw-guide).*

- **Freeze/thaw is the #1 concern.** Trails are rideable when frozen solid (early morning) but become mud pits when the surface thaws midday. The best window is 7-10 AM on cold, sunny days after several dry days.
- **Do NOT ride muddy trails.** Riding wet singletrack creates ruts that persist for months. If your tires leave impressions deeper than ~1 inch, turn around. This is a community ethic, not just a suggestion.
- **Check conditions morning-of.** Trail conditions can change overnight with a single freeze or rain event. Check Trailforks, call a local shop, or check Active SWV Facebook before heading out.
- **Dress in layers.** February mornings start at 18-28¬∞F but sunny afternoons can reach 48¬∞F. Wind chill on descents is significant. Bring windproof outer layer and warm gloves.
- **Carry microspikes.** If you need to hike any section, icy trail surfaces are common in shaded areas. Small microspikes clip to shoes easily.
- **Blaze orange at Babcock.** WV hunting seasons may extend into early-mid February. Wear blaze orange if riding at Babcock State Park.
- **Mountain biking in mid-February WV is opportunistic.** You may get zero rideable days during the trip. Plan bouldering as the primary activity and treat riding as a bonus when conditions allow.
